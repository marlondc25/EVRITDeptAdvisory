Everest IT Dept. Phishing Awareness Advisory

Protect Yourself from Phishing

Welcome to the Everest IT Department's Phishing Awareness Advisory. Phishing is a fraudulent attempt to obtain sensitive information, such as usernames, passwords, and credit card details, by disguising as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication. Stay informed and safeguard your personal and company data from malicious emails and viruses.

Tips to Identify Phishing Emails

Verify the sender's email address.
Be cautious with unsolicited attachments.
Check for generic greetings or urgent language.
Hover over links to preview the destination.
